Scoring note

Right now, 1 point = 1 correct exact score. Accuracy is calculated as points ÷ predictions made. Same goal-difference hits are manual bonus stats for close guesses, like predicting 2-1 when the real score was 1-0. Ranking order is: points first, then accuracy percentage, then goal-difference hits, then fewer predictions.
